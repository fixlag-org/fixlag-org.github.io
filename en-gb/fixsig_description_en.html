This symbol is composed of only eight characters, yet it richly represents the philosophy of fixlag.org.

The outer structures "◢◤◥◣" and "◥◢◣◤" are symmetrical yet misaligned, visually expressing lag, contradiction, and the gap between ideals and reality.

What emerges in the center is an upward arrow "↑", or an abstract image of a person quietly rising.

This fixsig symbolizes the will to rise from misalignment, the growth that dwells in waiting, and the sincerity of a human standing tall within constraints.

By attaching this symbol to email signatures, documents, websites, or business cards, we hope to quietly connect with those who resonate with our philosophy.
